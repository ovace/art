## Words

### 固定 gu4 ding4

Fixed, pre-determined without possibility of change, to fix (a date).

Fix date example:

    她把锻炼的时间固定在星期六下午

    Tā bǎ duànliànde shíjiān gùdìngzài xīngqīliùxiàwǔ.

    She fixed the exercise time for Saturday afternoons.

#### 被投入监狱 bei4 tou2 ru4 jian1 yu4
